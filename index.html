A B O U T

JOSH URBAN
From NYC to KC

I’m originally from New York, but now Kansas City is my home. In 2011 I trained with the stylists at Knotty Boy in Vancouver, BC. I am one of only a handful of Knotty Boy affiliate stylists in the world.

Feel free to contact me with any questions about maintenance or dreadlocks in general. We’re all in this together, let’s make your dreads look better than you ever thought possible!

Classic Dreadlocks

The traditional dreadlock service that stay tight from creation. From partial to full dread service.

Dread Maintenance

If your already have dreads and they are getting out of control, I will get them back into proper shape.

Blunt Ends

If the ends of your dreads starting to fray maybe it is time to get the ends blunted. Come have it performed professionally.

Repair and Reconstruction

If your dreads are starting to get loose, you may need to have them properly repaired by a trained dreadlock specialist.

Extensions

I will seamlessly add length to new or existing dreadlocks using only human hair and the same technique I use for new dreads. It can take "a while".
